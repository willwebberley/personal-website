Research

Autonomic Dissemination of Information in Online Social Networks

I'm a PhD student at the School of Computer Science, Cardiff University.

My research is to do with the dissemination of content and information in online social networks - in particular, Twitter. I am supervised by Dr Stuart Allen and Prof Roger Whitaker.

I am interested in how information is spread around through the act of retweeting. My work so far has focused on the properties and behaviour of retweets and the ways in which different structures of a user's local networks can affect the behaviour of the propagation of retweets within it.
